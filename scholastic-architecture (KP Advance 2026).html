New Additions Explained

  • Assessment (STP): This is the compass. It ensures that while we teach Concepts (CBCI), we are actually measuring understanding, not just memory. It feeds directly into Metacognition via self-assessment.
  • Enrichment Programmes: This acts as the "Application Layer." While the classroom builds the concepts, enrichment (e.g., CCAs, trips, projects) provides the messy, real-world context needed for true Synoptic Thinking.

The "Core" Engine

The "Classroom Pedagogy" box is where your teachers live daily. It combines the What (CBCI) and the How (CoT).

Key Synergy: Use AI to bridge Pedagogy and Assessment. E.g., "Use AI to generate a rubric that assesses the thinking process (CoT) not just the final output."
